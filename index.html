CEO / Founder

Khosi Mhayise

Khosi founded Aesthetic Gift from a love of thoughtful design and locally made treasures. With a background in styling and event curation, she turned her passion into a business that helps people celebrate life with beautiful, meaningful gifts.

Vision: To be the leading maker of handcrafted hampers that bring joy and connection to every occasion.
